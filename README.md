## About Me

I'm a **B.Tech Computer Science (Data Science) student at UPES Dehradun**, currently working as a **Summer Intern at Aprisity**, where I focus on data analysis, business analysis, and AI automation. I love living at the intersection of data and decision-making, transforming messy, raw datasets into clear narratives that drive real outcomes.

What genuinely excites me is **building intelligent systems that think and act**, whether that's training reinforcement learning agents, teaching models to recognize subtle patterns, or automating workflows so humans can focus on the work that matters. I treat every project as a chance to learn something new and ship something useful.

Beyond the models and metrics, I care about **clean problem-solving and continuous growth**. I'm always sharpening my craft on platforms like LeetCode, HackerRank, and Kaggle, and I'm constantly chasing the next idea worth building.

> Always learning. Always shipping. Always curious.
